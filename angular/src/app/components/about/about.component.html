Features for users

  • Users can browse the selection of products aided by the pagination buttons, the search bar, or the category selection bar on the left.
  • They can add items to the cart, whether they are logged in or not, but they must create an account and sign in to "buy" anything.
  • The cart icon on the top right will keep count of the number of items they have added to the cart.
  • In the cart, they can adjust the number of items they want, or remove all items of one kind before finishing their purchase.
  • Users can see their purchase history in the History page.
  • After a user is done, they may log out.

Features for Admins

  • An administrator of the site can do everything that a user can.
  • On the home page, an administrator can edit and delete products and categories.
  • All products will have buttons indicating those functions.
  • Categories can be edited or deleted by clicking on icon with the two down-arrows next to each category.
  • In the admin section, Administrators can: create new categories, create new products, view the purchasing history of each user, and view all of the purchases made on the site.
  • Categories can be parent or child categories. Parent categories are essentially folders. New categories must have a parent selected from the menu, otherwise it will be created as a parent category.
  • New products must have a name, price, image, description, and a category.
  • On the users info, and Admin can see the purchasing history of each user.
  • The History section has the full history of the site.
